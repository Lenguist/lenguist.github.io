My current research interests are LLM memory and interpretability and robotic manipulation.

I have previously worked on quadruped locomotion, text-guided image editing, optimizing real-time object detection, techniques for evaluating how LLMs handle long document summarization and generation, and grammar correction.

I believe the ultimate goal of AI research is creating systems that can improve quality of life by accelerating advances in research. I am specifically interested in how AI can help advance physical engineering (EE, MechE, Civil Eng).
